This means that the number of yellow flowers after the windstorm is still 8.So, the number of yellow flowers after windstorm = 8.Since all yellow flowers were standing, the number of yellow flowers before the windstorm was also 8.So, Y = 8.Using the relationship P = 2Y, we can find the number of pink flowers before the windstorm.P = 2 * 8 = 16.So, before the windstorm, Olivia had 16 pink flowers and 8 yellow flowers.The total number of flowers before the windstorm was P + Y = 16 + 8 = 24.After the windstorm, Olivia had "20 flowers left in total".This means the total number of flowers remaining after the windstorm is 20.We are given that all yellow flowers (8) were still standing. So, the remaining 20 flowers consist of the 8 yellow flowers and some pink flowers.Let P_remaining be the number of pink flowers remaining after the windstorm and Y_remaining be the number of yellow flowers remaining after the windstorm.We know Y_remaining = 8 and P_remaining + Y_remaining = 20.So, P_remaining + 8 = 20.P_remaining = 20 - 8 = 12.So, after the windstorm, Olivia has 12 pink flowers and 8 yellow flowers.Before the windstorm, Olivia had 16 pink flowers and 8 yellow flowers.We need to find out how many flowers the windstorm damaged.The number of damaged flowers is the difference between the total number of flowers before the windstorm and the total number of flowers after the windstorm.Total flowers before windstorm = 24.Total flowers after windstorm = 20.Number of damaged flowers = Total flowers before windstorm - Total flowers after windstormNumber of damaged flowers = 24 - 20 = 4.Alternatively, we can look at the number of damaged pink flowers and yellow flowers separately.Number of yellow flowers damaged = Number of yellow flowers before windstorm - Number of yellow flowers after windstorm = 8 - 8 = 0.Number of pink flowers damaged = Number of pink flowers before windstorm - Number of pink flowers after windstorm = 16 - 12 = 4.Total damaged flowers = Number of damaged yellow flowers + Number of damaged pink flowers = 0 + 4 = 4.The question is "How many flowers did the windstorm damage?".We have calculated that the windstorm damaged 4 flowers.
